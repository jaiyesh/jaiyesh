### 🧠 Who I Am

I'm a **full-stack AI engineer** — I build real products from model to production. Not just notebooks. Not just demos. Systems that actually run.

My background spans **petroleum engineering** (IIT ISM Dhanbad) and **modern AI/ML**, which gives me an unusual lens: I understand domain problems deeply *and* can architect the AI systems that solve them.

At **Zensar Technologies**, I lead AI/ML delivery across enterprise clients — computer vision pipelines, LLM integrations, agentic systems, deployed on H100 infrastructure with Docker and Azure OpenAI.

Outside of that, I'm **building and shipping [InterviewValley](https://interviewvalley.com)** — an AI-powered mock interview platform with real-time voice AI (STT → LLM → TTS), FastAPI backend, Next.js frontend, and native mobile via Capacitor.

I also **teach** — ML sessions for power industry clients, university guest lectures, and a YouTube channel where I've covered LLMs, petroleum data analytics, and practical AI from scratch.
